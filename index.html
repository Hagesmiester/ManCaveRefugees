// Since 50,000 BC

The Story

It starts the same way for every bloke. You're sitting in your chair - your chair - and you hear the words that have echoed through the ages: "We need to talk."

Your ancestors retreated to caves. Your grandfather had his shed. Your old man had the garage. You've got a gaming room, a workshop, a corner of the pub where the barman knows your order and asks no questions.

"We didn't choose the cave life. The cave life chose us."

Man-Cave Refugees was born from a simple truth: every married man on earth has had the exact same thought at the exact same time - "Is it just me?" It's not. It never was. There are millions of us. We just made it official.

Whether it's perimenopause turning your sweet wife into someone you don't recognise, a honey-do list longer than your will to live, or the quiet desperation of being told you're breathing too loud - we get it. No judgement. No advice you didn't ask for. Just solidarity, some laughs, and products for men who've tried everything else.
